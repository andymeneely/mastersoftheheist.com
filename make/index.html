Maker Edition

My hope is to create a Your Last Heist: Maker Edition for those who are willing to make some of the components themselves. We will provide a cheaper, minimal Maker Edition of the game on a separate TheGameCrafter storefront (coming soon). This smaller edition will include the cards, dice, and other parts that are difficult to make.

If you have access to a laser cutter at your friendly local maker space - and you'd be surprised how many are out there - you'll be able to download the plans and just cut out the pieces in wood. This will likely be cheaper than buying the fully-printed version, and will be made of wood! I'm currently working on this myself and I'm very excited about this prospect.

Full instructions will be posted here as this project progresses.
