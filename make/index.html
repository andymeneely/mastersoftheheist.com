Maker Edition

My hope is to create a Your Last Heist: Maker Edition for those who are willing to make some of the components themselves. In particular, if you have access to a laser cutter at your friendly local maker-space, you'll be able to download the plans and just cut out the pieces in wood. This will likely be cheaper than buying the fully-printed version, and will be made of wood! I'm currently working on this and I'm very excited about this prospect. Full instructions will be posted here.
