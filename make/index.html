Maker Edition

I am creating a Your Last Heist: Maker Edition for those who are willing to make some of the components themselves. We will provide a cheaper, minimal Maker Edition of the game on a separate TheGameCrafter storefront. This smaller edition will include the cards, dice, and other parts that are difficult to make.

If you have access to a laser cutter at your friendly local maker space - and you'd be surprised how many are out there - you'll be able to download the plans and just cut out the pieces in wood. This will likely be cheaper than buying the fully-printed version, and will be made of wood! I'm currently working on this myself and I'm very excited about this prospect.

The Maker edition of the game is what is currently under development (as of September 2017). You can find the files here in the repository.
