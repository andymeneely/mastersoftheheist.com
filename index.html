You are only as good as your final job.

Your Last Heist is a cooperative, multi-stage heist game. You are playing the role of a uniquely skilled criminal who works with other skilled criminals to quietly traverse the building and steal items, all while dealing with obstacles like closing gates, guards, locks, sensors, and cameras.

Choose Your Character.

Everyone has their own special part to play in a heist. You must use your character's abilities to their fullest extent to maximize your chance of success.

The game comes with 18 characters, 6 Amateurs and 12 pros. As you progress through scenarios, your character will be promoted, getting better abilities while maintaining your prior abilities.

Refine Your Skills.

Skills define the most powerful moves you have available to you on your turn. Your die roll will determine which actions are available. You always have your Character actions available, of course.

Every character gets two skills, and those skills get refined into better skills. The game has 18 skills, 6 Amateur Skills and 12 Pro skills.
